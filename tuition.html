Beginners And Advanced Students Welcome


At Coastal Guitar Tution, we welcome everyone regarless of age or skill level. It doesn't matter if you have never picked up a guitar in your life before or don't even own a guitar. We can supply you with an instrument and have you playing music in as little as 45 minutes. So your first lesson will cost you absolutely nothing and you can even use our guitars for your taster lesson.

We offer various ways to learn. Lessons can either be on a 1-2-1 basis, or you can have lessons as part of a group of up to 5 people. We can tailor your lessons to suit your needs or requirements. Lessons are in 45 minute blocks and start from 8am and run until 10pm, 7 days a week.

We don't just teach you how to play the guitar, We'll teach you music theory and have you understanding and exploring your fretboard. You will become familiar with scales, modes, tones, semitones, sharps, and flats. Most importantly, you will understand how to apply this to your fingers and make it resonate on the guitars fretboard.
